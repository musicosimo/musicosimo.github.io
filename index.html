Simo Ait Harma — Multiinstrumentalist

Simo Ait Harma is a Berlin-based musician and multi-instrumentalist blending North African traditions with contemporary sound.

Raised in Morocco, his music is rooted in Gnawa and Amazigh heritage, reimagined through a modern guitar-driven approach shaped in Berlin’s diverse music scene.

He performs, records, and collaborates across intercultural projects, and also teaches guitar, sharing music across generations.
